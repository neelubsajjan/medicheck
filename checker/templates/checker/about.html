About MediAI

AI-Powered Multimodal Healthcare System

MediAI is a pre-screening Clinical Decision Support System (CDSS) integrating voice, image, and manual symptom input with personalised ML-driven diagnosis and telemedicine support.

🎯

Multimodal Diagnosis

Analyses symptoms from voice, image, and manual input — all combined for the most accurate result.

👤

Patient Profiling

BMI, age, and gender are computed and used in every prediction cycle for personalised health insights.

💬

AI Chatbot

Conversational assistant that collects symptoms, explains results, and guides you to the right care.

🏥

Hospital Finder

AI-ranked hospital recommendations based on your predicted condition, severity, and location.

How It Works

1

Create Profile

Register with your name, age, gender, height and weight. BMI is computed automatically and used for personalised predictions.

2

Input Symptoms

Use manual selection, voice speech recognition, or image upload — mix and match for the best result.

3

AI Analysis

Six ML models (Logistic Regression, SVM, KNN, Random Forest, Naive Bayes, Decision Tree) analyse your symptoms with your patient profile.

4

Get Your Report

Predicted disease, confidence score, severity level, top alternative diagnoses, precautions, and personalised home care advice.

5

Take Action

Book a tele-consultation, find nearby hospitals, or chat with the AI assistant for further guidance and follow-up.

Medical Disclaimer
MediAI is an educational pre-screening tool only. It is not a certified medical device and does not provide a clinical diagnosis. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for any health decisions.
